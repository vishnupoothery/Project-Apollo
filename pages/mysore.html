MYSORE


DESCRIPTION

The historic settlement of Mysuru (which changed its name from Mysore in 2014) is one of South India's most enchanting cities, famed for its glittering royal heritage and magnificent monuments and buildings. Its World Heritage–listed palace brings most travellers here, but Mysuru is also rich in tradition with a deeply atmospheric bazaar district littered with spice stores and incense stalls. Ashtanga yoga is another drawcard and there are several acclaimed schools which attract visitors from across the globe.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

Sightseeing in Bangalore & visit to Mysore
Upon your arrival in Bangalore, you’ll be picked up for your journey to Mysore. On your way to the regal city, you will get to visit the famous Bangalore Palace and Iskcon Temple. After you arrive in Mysore, check in into your hotel and relax for a while.
In the evening, visit the Brindavan Gardens located behind the Krishnaraja Sagar Dam and spend a memorable time together. Later, return to your hotel for an overnight stay in Mysore.

DAY 2

From Mysore Palace to Brindavan Gardens, gear up to make the most of this day at the famous attractions of the city.
Gear up for your second day of thrilling experiences and embark on an exciting Mysore city tour that will take you through some of the most interesting and famous places in Mysore. From the majestic Chamundi Hills to the famous Nandi Statue and the Wax Museum, go on to explore the important landmarks of Mysore like that of Mysore Palace, Tipu Sultan’s Palace, Mysore Zoo, and St. Philomena's Church.
Continue to explore the astonishing tourist places around Mysore like that of Ranganatha Swamy Temple, and Ranganthittu Bird Sanctuary that will fill you with an awe by their charm. Later in the evening, spend some quality time with your friends, family, or your better half at the refreshing and historical Brindavan Gardens that is located behind the Krishnaraja Sagar Dam. After an eventful day, get back to the hotel and enjoy your overnight stay.

DAY 3

Your exquisite Mysore tour package ends today as you bid a hearty farewell to the charming city of Mysore and set back on a safe return journey.
Have a heart breakfast and proceed with the check-out process at the hotel in Mysore as this day marks the end of your 2 nights 3 days exquisite Mysore tour package. You will be transferred from Mysore to Bangalore wherein you can enjoy shopping at the famous Town Market. Later in the day, you will be dropped at the airport/railway station, from where you can embark on a safe return journey.
