OOTY


DESCRIPTION

Popularly called the ‘Queen of the Hills’, this bustling hill station has been one of the favourite holiday destinations of travelers. Initially established in the era of British India to serve as the summer headquarters of Madras government, Ooty was quick in transitioning into a hill station where people came to unwind and to escape into nature throughout the year. The establishment of numerous tea estates throughout the city and nearby hills make for a picturesque drive.

ITINERARY

DAY 1

As you arrive in Bangalore, start your Ooty Tour Package by being driven to the Queen of the Hills-Ooty. After a seven-hour long journey, check into your hotel and relax for the rest of the evening.

DAY 2

The beauty of Nilgiri Hills often has a disarming effect on its visitors. Wake up on the second morning of your Ooty Tour Package and start the day with a healthy breakfast.
Afterwards, get ready for a busy day of sightseeing that takes you to the quiet hamlet of Coonoor and visits places like Sim’s Park, Lam’s Rock, Dolphin Nose, Ketti Valley, Tea factory and Doddabetta Peak.
After your sightseeing, return to the hotel in Ooty for a well deserved night’s sleep.

DAY 3

A quick pit-stop in imperial Mysore
As you arrive on the final day of your Ooty Tour Package, start the day with breakfast and then proceed to check-out from your hotel in Ooty.
En route, stop by in Mysore for a few hours to explore its mighty history and regal past. Visit the Mysore Palace and serene Brindavan Garden to get a whiff of the royal aristocracy that was prevalent in Mysore.
Finally, finish your journey in Bangalore and take your flight/train back home
