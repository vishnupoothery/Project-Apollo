JAISALMER


DESCRIPTION

The Jewel of Rajasthan, Jaisalmer, packs everything Rajasthan represents- Forts, sand dunes, desert safari, and the spicy Rajasthani food. When in town, take a desert Safari and spend a night in the desert. Back in the city, you would travel through narrow lanes with Havelis and tiny houses coexisting with each other in harmony. Almost the whole city is carved out of the Golden-Honey Sandstone as the Fort and lends the name Golden City to Jaisalmer.

ITINERARY

We leave for desert which is around 40 km from hotel post Lunch. After reaching the spot you will spend some time with your camel over there, to become friend with your companion for the next 18 hours. Afterwards we will start our journey to the Sunset point. Enjoy the beautiful view of desert in the evening and after sunset there will be tea, snacks, bonfire and dinner. We spend the night sleeping under the stars. Mattresses & quilt will be provided by hotel. In the morning post breakfast we again head out for camel safari and after that we head back to hotel.
